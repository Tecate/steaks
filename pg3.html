What exactly do we do?

grade

Team Leadership

Datri Design Group will work with key stakeholders within the organization to ensure all steps and processes are implemented to create a turnkey project that can be maintained going forward. Through the development to final execution we function as an extension of your team to get you from point A to point B painlessly and seamlessly.

fingerprint

Brand Marketing

Our job is to understand your brands essence, and what it means to your customer. We will work with you to distill your vision and translate it into a retail program that deliver best results. We develop a plan of action that targets the right communication vehicle at the appropriate time to profile and leverage your brand effectively.

language

Strategic Partnerships

We research, identify and investigate new opportunities with other organizations to find mutually beneficial areas to grow your business through strategic collaborations. We develop and foster partnerships that will leverage your brand and create incremental revenue opportunities.

build

Product Development

When it comes to figuring out the right assortment, we consider all the details including sourcing, packaging, and final product production. Our experience spans across various categories such as menu and drink development, packaging, housewares and tabletop. We can design and package an exclusive custom program of products or menu items that deliver on your brand and resonate with your customer.

visibility

Visual Merchandising

We work with store designers and operation teams to ensure the brand essence that we developed in the products are then carried throughout every touch point the customer sees for effective merchandising solutions.

assignment

Market Planning

We know the devil is in the details and the strategy is only as good as how it's executed, this is why we work closely with your team to ensure we operationalize and communicate every part of the plan for go to market success.
